Saturday Morning Pancakes - WIP

These amounts feed the 3 of us with a couple leftover to snack on later.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup milk
  • 1 tbsp apple cider vinegar
  • 1 1/2 cups white flour
  • 1/2 cup wheat flour
  • 2 tsp baking powder
  • 1 tsp baking soda
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1 tbsp sugar
  • 1 tsp cardamom
  • 2 eggs
  • 2 tbsp canola oil
  • 1/2 cup plain yogurt and 1/2 water (could use milk, amounts can vary but should add up to 1 cup)
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract

Directions

Combine milk and apple cider vinegar and set aside.

Combine dry ingredients.

Beat together eggs and oil. Add yogurt, water, vanilla, and milk vinegar mixture. Mix wet and dry ingredients and stir lightly. I like to fry them using coconut oil and top them with butter, maple syrup, fruit, and plain yogurt.
