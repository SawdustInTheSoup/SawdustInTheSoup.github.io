Split Pea Soup - WIP

Ingredients

  • 2 tbsp bacon grease
  • 1 onion coarsely chopped
  • 2 carrots coarsely chopped
  • 2 celery stalks coarsely chopped
  • 1/2 tsp white pepper
  • 1/2 tsp mustard powder or crushed mustard seed
  • 2 tsp apple cider vinegar
  • 2 cups dry split peas, rinsed
  • 6 cups boiling water
  • Salt, lawry's, fish sauce, and or soy to taste

Directions

I make mine in a pressure cooker but that step could be replaced by simmering on the stove for 30 minutes.

Sautee the onion, carrots, and celery in the bacon grease. Add the white pepper and mustard. When the bottom of the pan gets a little brown add the vinegar and scrape the bottom with the spatula. Add The peas and boiling water. Cook on high pressure 10 minutes. Blend in a Cuisinart in 2 or 3 batches and finish in a pot on the stove top. Simmer 10 more minutes or so stirring and adding salt and water to taste.
